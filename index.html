Drawing Hangman
A drawing guessing game, similar to Hangman but people have to guess the object being drawn within the amount of strokes it takes to draw the Hangman or they lose.

HOW TO PLAY

  • One person is the one drawing the word, and the rest of the players are the ones guessing

  • Each player can make a guess as to what the drawing is after each stroke

  • If the players guess wrong, then the person drawing adds a body part to the hangman

  • If the players guess your drawing before the hangman is complete, you lose

  • If the players are not successful in guessing, you win

SETTING UP THE GAME

  • The set-up for this game is similar to the classic Hangman set-up

    • Begin by drawing the outline of where the "hangman" will go


  • Then, just follow the instructions to the left and add parts to the hangman as you go on.
